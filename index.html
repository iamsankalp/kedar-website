Lead Product DesignerKedar Nimkar


I care about: Form, function, context, consistency, music, poetry, people, honesty, design & simplicity.

Since the past 6 years I have been making travel simple at Cleartrip. Prior to that, I was designing product experiences at a boutique agency PaperPlane. My driveway to the product world came from Webchutney & burrp

When I'm not designing (which hardly happens), most of my time is invested in poetry, magic & football. I read, write and spend a lot of time with Kiddo (my dog). Connect with me on Twitter or Medium but I barely exist on Instagram and Facebook (for patterns & design).

Work : 12 years

2016 Cleartrip Local

Launched 4 new products catering to things to do in the city when users are not travelling. "I am author of a copyrighted interaction element. Cleartrip got featured in App store and Apple website"

2015 Mobile makeover & Cleartrip Activities

Improved mobile web experience to give users freedom from downloading the app. Launched Activities as a pilot project which then matured to be Cleartrip Local.

2014 Weekend Getaways & Collections

Launched 2 new properties for Hotel booking users who wish to unwind on weekends near the city.

2013 Cleartrip Tuxedo

All new look to Cleartrip search experience for Flights, hotels & packages. This was one of the biggest projects at Cleartrip in last 10 years

2012 All new Cleartrip booking experience

Reimagined the core booking experience for flights, hotels, trains & other products. It was one of the most sensitive projects as this impacted conversion

2011 Ticket & email redesign

One of the most user friendly, clean and simple e-ticket layout and emails that are sent pre and post booking.

2010 Bluefrog @PaperPlane

blueFROG is a revolutionary integrated music project which provides platform for music artists to perform. I launched the site in 3 cities with events calendar, artist profiles and more.

2009 Multiple clients @PaperPlane

2009 Multiple clients @Webchutney

This is where I found my real drive for products working on a wide range of products — Flykingfisher, Titan, Autocar India, CampaignIndia, Diageo, NCPA and many more micro sites.
